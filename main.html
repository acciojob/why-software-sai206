Why I want to become a developer?

Problem-solving opportunities: Engineering presents a multitude of complex challenges that require innovative problem-solving skills. As an engineer, you'll have the chance to tackle real-world problems and develop creative solutions that can make a positive impact on society. Creating and shaping the future: Engineers have the power to shape the world around them. Through designing new technologies, infrastructure, and systems, engineers contribute to advancements in various fields such as transportation, communication, healthcare, energy, and more. Being an engineer allows you to be at the forefront of progress and contribute to building a better future. Intellectual stimulation: Engineering is a field that demands continuous learning and intellectual growth. The work is dynamic, diverse, and constantly evolving, ensuring that you'll always be engaged in stimulating and challenging projects. The opportunity to continuously expand your knowledge and skills can be incredibly rewarding. Teamwork and collaboration: Engineering projects often require teamwork and collaboration with professionals from different disciplines. As an engineer, you'll have the opportunity to work in diverse teams, learning from and collaborating with experts in various areas. This fosters personal growth, broadens perspectives, and enhances interpersonal skills.
